SafePassage documentation
==========================

`README (한국어) <https://github.com/search5/safe-passage/blob/main/README.ko.md>`_

`SafePassage <https://github.com/search5/safe-passage>`_ is a secure,
lightweight `Obsidian <https://obsidian.md>`_ plugin that links your local
KeePass databases (``.kdbx``) to your vault, rendering masked credential
chips and structured tables directly inside your notes — without ever
storing your master passwords or secrets in plain text.
